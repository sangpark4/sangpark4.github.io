응용 수학 및 기계 공학 학사

2021년 졸업 예정
Kingston, Ontario, Canada

Queen’s University에서 공부하는 동안 수학과 기계 공학에 대한 기본적인 지식을 얻음과 동시에 특히 응용 수학 프로그램은 창의적으로 문제에 접근하는 방식을 익히는데 큰도움이 되었으며, 이 과정동한 팀워크와 의사 소통 능력을 발휘하여 성공적으로 프로젝트를 수행하였습니다.

대학에서 응용 수학 및 기계 공학을 전공하며 기계 설계, 운동학, 기계 역학 그리고 기계 진동학 등의 기계 공학 코스와 실해석학, 대수 구조 그리고 복소해석학등의 응용 수학 코스를 이수하며 추상대수학의 기본적 개념을 공부하였고 확률과 실험 데이터 관리등의 코스를 통하여 데이터 분석에 대한 관심이 생겼습니다. 엔지니어링 실혐과정을 통하여 책에서 공부한 이론을 적용하고 분석하는 기술을 습득하였습니다.

Gearbox Project

A gearbox system was designed to maximize the torque and speed of a small car. The system was a shifting one-stage gear network that consisted of gears, pinions, input and output shafts, bearings, and housing. After calculations regarding the number of teeth and diameter of the gears and pinions, the system was modelled using Siemens NX and prepared for 3D printing.

Controlling the pH Levels of Drinking Water

An unknown "black box" system was analyzed and controlled for this project. By using principles of control theory, the system was designed to control the quality of drinking water. In order to achieve this, we analyzed the controllability and stability of the system. Some of the tools that were used for this project include PID-controllers, Bode and Nyquist plots, transfer functions, and the linear time-invariance property.

Removal of Plastics in Sewage Systems

A filtration system was designed to eliminate macro and microplastics in drainage and sewage systems. The designed solution used a dissolved air flotation process to effectively eliminate plastic in wastewater before it enters the treatment plant.

Sexual Assault Centre Kingston Guidance Mobile Application

A mobile application was made using JavaScript and Android Studio in order to provide guidance to victims and educate other young adults. The target audience for this project were those aged 16 to 24, which was requested by Sexual Assault Centre Kingston.

The app consisted of four main sections: Resources, Scenarios, Consent, and Direct Questions. The Resources section was designed to provide the users with several helplines and sources. The Scenarios section is a quiz section that places the user in an observer's point of view where they witness an event taking place. The user would then choose an action they might take in order to help the victim. The Direct Questions section leads the user through a specific set of events that might have occurred to them. As the user specifies the situation in more depth, the app will guide them to a resource that is suited for their circumstances. Finally, the Consent section provides general information regarding consent and the laws surrounding it.

Mars Colony

A sustainable Mars Colony was designed using the resources that are already present on the planet and the few that can be brought from Earth. The modelling of this design was split into four main sections: Space Travel, Mining and Mineral Processing, Agriculture and Water, and Power. The amount of propellant and optimal route for travel was analyzed. Both the mining process and the food and water supply were also analyzed. Finally, the power supply to provide for approximately 1000 humans was also designed.
